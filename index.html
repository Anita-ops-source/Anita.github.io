What BriefIQ does

Leaders don’t need more slides — they need clarity. BriefIQ summarizes complex information into concise, structured outputs: what changed, what matters, what to do next, and what could go wrong.

Faster alignment

Executive-ready briefs

One-page summaries that highlight the decision, the trade-offs, and the recommendation.

Fewer surprises

Risk + assumptions

Surfacing hidden assumptions, constraints, and potential failure points before you commit.

Less busywork

Action extraction

Turns long notes into owners, deadlines, next steps, and follow-up questions.

How it works

Upload or paste raw information. BriefIQ organizes it into a standard executive template: context → insights → options → recommendation → risks → next actions.

Step 1

Bring the input

Meeting transcript, status report, KPI dashboard notes, or a messy email thread.

Step 2

Choose the output

Executive summary, action list, stakeholder update, or a “what changed” brief.

Step 3

Review + refine

Ask follow-ups, request a different tone, or generate a final decision memo.
